An Authentic Vegetarian Feast in Shalimar Bagh

Located in Shalimar Bagh's CC Block Market, Spice Sea Restaurant has become a trusted local culinary landmark. We believe vegetarian food should be celebratory, colorful, and packed with spices. We maintain separate hygiene standards and use cold-pressed oils and high-grade flour.

Whether you are dining in with family for lunch, calling in a fast-food delivery, or booking our private air-conditioned banquet hall for a social gathering, we promise consistent, delicious results.
